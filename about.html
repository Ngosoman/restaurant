About Us

We are simply just a bunch of food lovers who accidentally turned our cravings into a business. Here we believe in cheesy jokes, saucy slices and even crust that deserves it's own fan club. We serve food that makes your taste buds do a happy dance and your diet takes a short vacation. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a full-on feast, just know- your cravings are in dangerously good hands.

We are Located at Wallstreet Road the second turn after the round-about.
